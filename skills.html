Commerce Skills Roadmap

Skills that actually decide your career growth — not just degrees

Core Skills Every Commerce Student Must Learn

  • Excel & Google Sheets (absolute must)
  • Basic accounting & financial understanding
  • Communication & presentation skills
  • Analytical thinking & problem solving
  • Professional email & workplace etiquette

Technical & Career-Boosting Skills

  • Financial analysis & interpretation
  • Basic data analysis (Excel + charts)
  • Understanding business models
  • Tax basics & compliance awareness
  • AI tools for productivity (research, notes, planning)

Skills by Career Stage

  1. College Level: Excel, communication, basics of finance
  2. Professional Course Stage: Analytical thinking, subject depth
  3. Early Career: Decision-making, presentation, confidence

Free Learning Resources

  • Excel Basics — Microsoft Learn
  • Accounting Basics — Khan Academy
  • Communication Skills — Coursera (Free options)

Common Mistakes Students Make

  • Chasing degrees without skills
  • Ignoring Excel and practical tools
  • Waiting until final year to upskill
  • Copying others without self-assessment

Strong skills create strong careers — regardless of the course.
