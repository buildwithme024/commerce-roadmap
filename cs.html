Company Secretary (CS) Roadmap

Corporate law, compliance & governance career

Who Should Choose CS?

  • Students interested in law & corporate rules
  • Those who like documentation & precision
  • Students aiming board-level corporate roles

CS Exam Roadmap

  • Foundation
  • Executive
  • Professional

Must-Learn Skills

  • Corporate law interpretation
  • Drafting & compliance filings
  • Attention to detail
  • Professional communication

Career Options

  • Company Secretary
  • Compliance officer
  • Corporate legal advisor
  • Consulting & practice

Salary Reality

  • Fresh CS: ₹6–10 LPA
  • Experienced / Practice: ₹15+ LPA

Common Mistakes

  • Underestimating legal depth
  • Poor drafting skills
  • Lack of practical exposure

Should You Choose CS?

Choose if:

  • You enjoy law & compliance

Avoid if:

  • You dislike rules & documentation

CS careers reward precision and trust.
