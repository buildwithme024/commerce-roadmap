B.Com Roadmap

What to learn, what to avoid, and how to use B.Com smartly

Year 1 – Build Foundations

  • Financial Accounting basics
  • Business Economics
  • Basic Excel (formulas, charts)
  • Communication & presentation skills

Year 2 – Skill Development

  • Corporate Accounting
  • GST fundamentals
  • Advanced Excel (Pivot Tables, Lookups)
  • Internships & practical exposure

Year 3 – Career Direction

  • Income Tax basics
  • Financial analysis & reporting
  • Decide next step: CA / MBA / Job
  • Resume + interview preparation

Career Options After B.Com

  • Accounts Executive — ₹3–6 LPA
  • Junior Accountant — ₹3–5 LPA
  • Business Analyst (with skills) — ₹5–8 LPA
  • Higher studies: CA, CMA, CS, MBA

Skills That Multiply B.Com Value

  • Excel & data handling
  • Accounting software (Tally / ERP basics)
  • Professional communication
  • Basic financial modeling

Free Learning Resources

  • Excel & Business Tools – Microsoft Learn
  • Accounting & Finance – Khan Academy
  • Commerce Concepts – CA Rachana Phade (YouTube)

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Relying only on college syllabus
  • Ignoring Excel & practical skills
  • Delaying career decisions till final year
  • Copying others without clarity

Designed to give commerce students clarity, not confusion.
