Chartered Accountant (CA) Roadmap

The CA course is divided into three stages. Each stage demands different skills and discipline.

Stage 1: CA Foundation

  • Accounting Basics
  • Business Laws
  • Mathematics & Logical Reasoning
  • Economics
  • Time management & exam strategy

Stage 2: CA Intermediate

  • Advanced Accounting
  • Corporate & Other Laws
  • Cost & Management Accounting
  • Taxation (GST + Income Tax)
  • Practical problem-solving

Stage 3: CA Final

  • Financial Reporting
  • Strategic Financial Management
  • Audit & Professional Ethics
  • Direct & International Taxation
  • Decision-making under pressure

Is CA Right for You?

  1. Can you study consistently for 2–3 years?
  2. Are you okay with delayed rewards?
  3. Do you handle failure without quitting?
  4. Can you stay disciplined without motivation?

If most answers are “yes”, CA fits you.
