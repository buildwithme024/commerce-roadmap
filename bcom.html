B.Com Career Roadmap

Build foundations and unlock multiple career paths

What is B.Com?

Bachelor of Commerce builds fundamentals in accounting, finance, business and economics.

Skills to Learn During B.Com

  • Accounting fundamentals
  • Advanced Excel
  • Business communication
  • Basic data analysis

Career Options After B.Com

  • Accountant
  • Financial Analyst
  • MBA / CA / CMA / CS
  • Banking & government exams

Salary Expectations

  • Freshers: ₹3–6 LPA
  • With skills & experience: ₹8–12 LPA+
B.Com alone is not enough. Skills decide growth.
