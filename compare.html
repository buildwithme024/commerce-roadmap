Who Should Choose What?

Choose B.Com if:

  • You want flexibility and time to explore
  • You plan CA, MBA, CFA, or analytics later
  • You want lower pressure early on

Choose BBA if:

  • You want management exposure early
  • You aim for MBA from a good college
  • You’re interested in leadership roles

Choose CA if:

  • You can handle long-term pressure
  • You want top-tier finance credibility
  • You’re disciplined and consistent

Choose CS if:

  • You’re interested in law & corporate governance
  • You want boardroom-level roles
  • You prefer theory + compliance work

Choose CMA if:

  • You like strategy, costing & performance analysis
  • You want roles inside companies (not audit-heavy)

Choose MBA if:

  • You’re ready to invest time & money
  • You aim for leadership or fast growth
  • You can get into a decent college

Important Truths

  • Higher difficulty ≠ guaranteed success
  • Skills matter more than degrees
  • Early clarity saves years of regret

Choose wisely. Time is your most expensive asset.
